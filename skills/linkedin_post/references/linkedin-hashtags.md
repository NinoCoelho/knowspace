### LinkedIn vs Instagram
| Aspect | Instagram | LinkedIn |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Quantity | 15-30 | 5-10 |
| Strategy | Volume + reach | Quality + relevance |
| Mix | Popular + niche + brand | 60% popular + 30% niche + 10% brand |

## Performance Optimization

### Testing Strategy
1. Test different hashtag combinations
2. Track engagement rates
3. Identify top performers
4. Adjust mix based on results

### Weekly Review
- Review top 3 posts of the week
- Note which hashtags performed best
- Increase use of high performers
- Test 1-2 new hashtags

### Monthly Analysis
- Full hashtag performance review
- Remove low-performing hashtags
- Add new tested hashtags
- Update strategy based on data

## Best Practices

### Quantity
- ✅ 5-10 hashtags per post
- ❌ 15+ hashtags (looks spammy)
- ❌ 1-2 hashtags (low reach)

### Relevance
- ✅ Hashtags relevant to content
- ✅ Mix of popular + niche
- ❌ Random popular hashtags
- ❌ Irrelevant trending hashtags

### Placement
- ✅ At the end of post
- ✅ In a single line
- ❌ In the middle of content
- ❌ Multiple scattered lines

### Formatting
- ✅ #PascalCase (not #pascalcase)
- ✅ #BusinessStrategy (readable)
- ❌ #businessstrategy (hard to read)
- ❌ #BUSINESSSTRATEGY (hard to read)

## Hashtags to Avoid

### Overused (Too Generic)
- #Business (use sparingly)
- #Success (too vague)
- #Motivation (not professional)
- #Life (not relevant)
- #Love (not professional)

### Irrelevant (Avoid)
- Hashtags not related to your industry
- Hashtags not related to your content
- Hashtags that mislead readers
- Trending hashtags just for visibility
