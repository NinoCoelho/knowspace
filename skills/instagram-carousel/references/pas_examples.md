# PAS Framework Examples

## What is PAS?

PAS = Problem - Agitation - Solution

A copywriting framework that:
1. Identifies a problem your audience faces
2. Agitates the pain (makes it feel real and urgent)
3. Presents your solution as the answer

## Structure

### Problem (Slide 1)
- State the problem clearly
- Make it relatable to your audience
- Keep text short (≤6 words on image)

### Agitation (Slide 2)
- Amplify the pain
- Add data or specific examples
- Show consequences of inaction

### Solution (Slide 3)
- Present your solution
- Highlight key benefits
- Show transformation

### CTA (Slide 4)
- Clear call to action
- Portrait image
- Direct instruction

## Example 1: Business Pain Point

**Problem:** "Still doing [task] manually?"
**Agitation:** "Costs 10hrs/week. Errors up 30%. Competitors automated last year."
**Solution:** "Our system automates in 2hrs. Zero errors. 5x faster."
**CTA:** "DM 'DEMO' for free trial"

## Example 2: Industry Challenge

**Problem:** "Margins shrinking in [industry]?"
**Agitation:** "Input costs +15%. Competition +20%. Price pressure relentless."
**Solution:** "Our clients see 25% margin improvement in 90 days."
**CTA:** "DM 'MARGIN' for case study"

## Example 3: Growth Opportunity

**Problem:** "Stuck at [current level]?"
**Agitation:** "Same revenue 3 years. Team burned out. No clear path forward."
**Solution:** "Our framework: 3x growth in 12 months. Proven 50+ times."
**CTA:** "DM 'SCALE' to start"

## Caption Template

```
[HOOK - Problem statement as question or bold claim]

[PROBLEM PARAGRAPH]
Describe the problem in relatable terms. Make it personal.

[AGITATION PARAGRAPH]
Add data, statistics, or specific examples that amplify the pain.
Show what happens if they don't act.

[SOLUTION PARAGRAPH]
Present your solution. Be specific about results and benefits.
Include social proof if available.

[CTA]
Clear, direct action. One specific thing to do.

[HASHTAGS]
Mix of popular, niche, brand, and location hashtags.
```

## Tips

1. **Be specific** - "Losing $10k/month" > "Losing money"
2. **Use data** - Numbers make pain tangible
3. **Keep it simple** - One problem, one solution
4. **Make CTA clear** - Exactly what to do next
5. **Test hooks** - First line determines scroll-stop
