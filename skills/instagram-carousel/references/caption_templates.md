## Formatting Best Practices

### Line Breaks
- Use double line breaks between sections
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
- Mobile-friendly: break after 50-60 characters

### Emoji Usage
- Strategic, not excessive
- Maximum 3-5 per caption
- Use for emphasis, not decoration

### First Line
- Most important line
- Appears in preview
- Should create curiosity or state value

### Length Guidelines
- Carousel: 150-300 words
- Single post: 100-200 words
- Hook: Under 125 characters

### CTA Patterns
- Direct: "DM 'WORD' for [benefit]"
- Question: "Ready to [outcome]? DM me."
- Urgency: "Limited spots. DM now."
- Social: "Follow for more [topic] tips"

## Hashtag Strategy

### Mix Formula
- 40% popular (high volume)
- 30% niche (specific to topic)
- 20% brand (your company/personal)
- 10% location (if relevant)

### Count
- Instagram: 20-30 hashtags
- Place at end of caption
- Can also put in first comment

### Popular Types
- Industry: #business #entrepreneur #ceo
- Topic: #[your industry] #[your niche]
- Location: #[city] #[country]
- Brand: #[your brand] #[your name]
