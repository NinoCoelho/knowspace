## Best Practices

1. **Hooks in first 2 lines** (mobile preview)
2. **Use paragraphs** (not bullets like Instagram)
3. **Be data-driven** (specific numbers)
4. **Single, clear CTA** (not multiple CTAs)
5. **5-10 hashtags** (quality over quantity)
6. **Professional tone** (B2B audience)
7. **Strategic emojis** (not excessive)

---

## Character Limits

- **Maximum:** 3,000 characters (LinkedIn limit)
- **Optimal:** 2,000 characters (best engagement)
- **Minimum:** 1,000 characters (for long-form)
- **Hook:** 150 characters max (first 2 lines)

---

## Formatting Tips

### Line Breaks
- Use blank lines between sections
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 lines)
- Add space before hashtags

### Emojis
- Use strategically (✅, 📊, 💡, 🚀)
- Don't overuse (max 5-7 per post)
- Place at start of list items

### Numbers
- Spell out small numbers (one, two, three)
- Use digits for statistics (37%, $5M, 1,000)
- Use digits for lists (1️⃣, 2️⃣, 3️⃣)
